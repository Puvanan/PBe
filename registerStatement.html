E-Statement Service

Register now for the E-Statement Service, which will enable you to obtain an electronic version of your Current or Credit / Debit Card Account Statement. This E-Statement is in virtually identical in format as your original paper-based statement which you can view, save and print out at your convenience. In addition, these E-Statement will be kept online up to 6 months for your easy reference (beginning from the date of registration). All you need is to register for this service and view your E-Statements from the next statement cycle onwards.

Note:
  • To view and print E-Statement, you must have Adobe Acrobat version 5 or above installed.
  • Your monthly paper-based statement will cease to be sent to you after registration for the E-Statement Service.
  • Customers who register for this service agree to be bound by the TERMS AND CONDITIONS governing the service.
  • This function is subject to the Terms and Conditions governing the E-Statement Service. The initial use of the service shall be deemed as your agreement to be bound by these terms and conditions and as your acknowledgement and acceptance of all the inherent risks associated with the use of the service.
  • Upon generation of your E-Statement, the Bank will cease sending paper based statement to you. For statement request(s) for a specific period select Account Inquiry / Statement Request / Hard-copy Statement.
  • If you wish to download Acrobat Reader please go to the following site and follow the on-screen instructions : http://www.adobe.com/products/acrobat/readstep.html
