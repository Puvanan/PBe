Note
  • The new transfer service is applicable to funds transfer to other banks within Malaysia only. Funds will be debited from the applicant's account and credited into the beneficiary's account immediately.
  • The service is only available during our operating hours 12:05 a.m. - 12 midnight.
  • The third party fund transfer limit is RM20,000.00 (PAC) and RM50,000.00 (SecureSign).
  • The limit for a single transaction is at a maximum value of RM10,000.00 per transaction using PAC.
  • New Transfers will not be initiated if Beneficiary's Bank rejects or is unable to confirm the transaction into the particular account. During such occurrences, the funds will be credited back into your account.
  • The default third party transaction limit is RM3,000.00. This daily limit can be increased or decreased by selecting Profile Maintenance / Manage Profile / Change Daily Limit.
